Medical keys extraction never been easier before.

Why should I use this tool?

Turning unstructured data such as text in research can help us obtain important insights in to your data to help aid strategic business decisions. Traditionally this involve individuals manually tagging or coding large data sets which can be time consuming, complex not to mention boring.
This tool is to help you to classify specifically medical research text to ICD10 classification blocks and chapters. We make classification quick, fun and easy to use so your staff can get on with more important things.

The tool can be used in 3 simple steps:


1. Organise your research papers so that you have the title summary and abstract stored in an excel or CSV file. The screen shot below give you a sample what you spreadsheet should look like. The file can handle upto 10,000 records.
2. Upload the data and make a coffee whilst waiting for your data to be prepared. Our special algorithm will add predicted chapter and block names to your research data.
3. Our machine offers 70% accuracy. If you are happy with this simply download the data and you're done. We recommend for extra accuracy, use our easy to use validation tool to help guide you to validate and check your predictions.
